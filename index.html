Overview

What is Digital Shadow?

A systematic engine that captures, stores, and reconstructs the timeline of your digital life — every event, every action, every moment.

⚡

The Problem

Every day, you generate thousands of digital events — phone unlocks, messages, payments, GPS movements. But human memory forgets the sequence, the timing, and the connections between these moments. Critical patterns are lost forever.

🧠

The Solution

Digital Shadow uses a Linked List to store events in chronological order and a Stack to enable undo and replay capabilities. It reconstructs a complete, navigable timeline of your digital footprint with full analytical power.

🎯

Live Demo

Experience the system in action. Add events, navigate timelines, undo actions, replay entire days, and view statistical breakdowns — all through an elegant console-inspired interface built right into your browser.

Process

How It Works

Three simple steps to reconstruct your digital timeline.

1

Record

Every digital action — from phone unlocks to payments — is captured as a structured event with timestamp, category, and metadata.

→
2

Store

Events are inserted into a doubly linked list, maintaining chronological order. A stack keeps track of recent actions for undo capability.

→
3

Reconstruct

Traverse the linked list to rebuild the complete timeline. Analyze patterns, replay sequences, and gain insights into your digital behavior.
